The venue is `Tsubogawa 204 Meeting Room` in the same building as the technical college at ([Google Map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/TkXkrvhXdeTjNbwF7), GPS: 26.205776110763367, 127.68035664117075).

Please go around to the back side of the building and enter from the second-floor entrance.

## Entrance Procedure

### 1. Go to the back-side entrance on the second floor

Address: `3-5-3 Tsubogawa, Naha, Okinawa`

There is a door with a sign for the second-floor entrance at the back of the building.

Enter the password for the smart lock, then press the check button to unlock the entrance door. The password will be shared separately with participants.

### 2. Enter Room 204

At the door of `Tsubogawa 204 Meeting Room`, enter the room password and press the `Remotelock` button to unlock the room. The password will be shared separately with participants.

After entering, please turn on the lights and use the room.
